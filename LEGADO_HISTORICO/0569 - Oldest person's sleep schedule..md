### ASSISTENTE

Jeanne Calment, who holds the record as the oldest person ever, lived to be 122 years and 164 days. She reportedly maintained a consistent sleep schedule, averaging about 7 to 8 hours of sleep per night. This aligns with general recommendations for older adults, which suggest they need around 7 to 8 hours of sleep nightly for optimal health【7†source】【8†source】. 

Calment's lifestyle, which included regular sleep, a balanced diet rich in olive oil and chocolate, and light physical activity like cycling and fencing, contributed to her remarkable longevity【8†source】.
