### ASSISTENTE

The ILSC English Test and its resources provide a few strategies to help you achieve your desired score and understand the evaluation process better.

1. **Understanding the Tests and Levels**: ILSC offers various tests, such as the FREE English Test, the GENERAL English Placement Test, and the ACADEMIC English/French Placement Test. Each test assesses different skills, including listening, reading, speaking, grammar, and writing. For example, the FREE English Test focuses on listening and reading skills and provides instant results with study recommendations to help you prepare effectively. Meanwhile, the GENERAL and ACADEMIC Placement Tests offer a broader evaluation, including a speaking assessment that is manually graded by expert assessors. Results for these tests are typically provided within 24 hours, allowing you to quickly adjust your study plans as needed [6].

2. **Creating a Study Plan**: ILSC recommends developing a study plan tailored to your current level and goals. You can start by taking the free online assessment to gauge your proficiency level. Use the provided study recommendations to create a structured plan that breaks your study time into manageable shifts. Mapping your study schedule on a calendar can also help you track your progress and ensure you dedicate sufficient time to each topic [6][7].

3. **Study Tips for Success**: The ILSC blog outlines several practical tips to help you prepare for exams, such as:
   - Pre-organizing your study materials and notes.
   - Developing a study plan with time allocations for each topic.
   - Choosing an effective study environment free from distractions.
   - Taking breaks to stay refreshed and avoiding studying on an empty stomach [8].

4. **Additional Resources**: ILSC suggests utilizing their resources, such as the online English test and various academic preparation programs, to further enhance your learning journey. This preparation can include taking classes that focus on specific skills (e.g., IELTS preparation) or integrating various focus areas like Business English or Creative and Cultural English [6][7].

For more detailed information and to take their free online test, you can visit their website: [ILSC Free Online English Test](https://www.ilsc.com/free-online-english-test)【7†source】.

Cada teste avalia habilidades específicas, incluindo compreensão auditiva, leitura, fala, gramática e escrita.
