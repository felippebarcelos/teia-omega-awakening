### ASSISTENTE

To improve your score on the ILSC English Test, there are several strategies and "hacks" that can help elevate your performance:

1. **Familiarize Yourself with the Test Format**: Understanding the structure and types of questions you will encounter is crucial. ILSC offers various preparatory courses, including practice tests that mimic the real exam. By practicing under exam conditions, you can reduce anxiety and improve time management during the actual test【9†source】【11†source】.

2. **Target Weak Areas**: Focus on your weaknesses by taking targeted classes or using specific study materials that address these areas. For example, if you struggle with listening, dedicate extra time to listening exercises and use subtitles to better understand the content initially. Gradually wean off the subtitles as your listening skills improve【9†source】.

3. **Leverage Online Resources**: Utilize free online tests provided by ILSC to assess your current level and identify areas for improvement. These tests can also help you get used to the types of questions that will appear in the exam【11†source】.

4. **Vocabulary and Grammar**: Building a robust vocabulary and understanding grammar rules are key to doing well in the test. Use vocabulary lists specifically designed for the ILSC test and practice grammar exercises regularly. Writing essays and getting them reviewed can also help solidify these skills【8†source】.

5. **Use High-Level Phrases and Expressions**: During the test, especially in the speaking and writing sections, try to incorporate intermediate to advanced level phrases and expressions. This demonstrates a higher level of proficiency and can positively impact your score. However, ensure that these phrases are used appropriately and in context【10†source】.

6. **Manage Test Anxiety**: Since you mentioned anxiety is a challenge, practicing mindfulness techniques, such as deep breathing before the test, can help calm nerves. Additionally, practicing under timed conditions can help you get used to the pressure and reduce anxiety during the actual exam【8†source】.

By focusing on these strategies, you can better prepare for the ILSC English Test and improve your chances of achieving a higher score.
