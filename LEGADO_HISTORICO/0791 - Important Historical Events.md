# 0791 - Important Historical Events

### USUÁRIO

escreva como um estudante intermediário 3 essa pergunta:


---

### ASSISTENTE

Claro! Por favor, me diga qual pergunta você gostaria de formular.

---

### USUÁRIO

What do you think is one of the most important events in history? How would your life be different if this event had not happened? What do you think we can learn from this historical event?

---

### ASSISTENTE

How would you rewrite this question at an Intermediate 3 level? Here is a suggestion:

"What is an important event in history for you? How would your life be different if it never happened? What can we learn from this event?"

---

### USUÁRIO

preciso de uma resposta

---

### ASSISTENTE

Here’s a possible response at an Intermediate 3 level:

"One of the most important events in history, in my opinion, is the invention of the internet. If this event had not happened, my life would be very different. I would not be able to easily access information, connect with people around the world, or work online. Without the internet, many opportunities would not exist. We can learn from this event that technology has the power to change the world and our lives in many ways, both positive and negative. It reminds us to use technology wisely and think about its impact on our society."

---

### USUÁRIO

agora Describe a story that you enjoy. It can be a book, a movie, a TV show - any story. What do you like best about the story? How do you think it could be better?

---

### ASSISTENTE

Here’s a possible response at an Intermediate 3 level:

"I really enjoy the story of *Harry Potter*, which is a series of books and movies. It is about a young wizard who goes to a magical school and fights against dark forces. What I like best about the story is the world of magic and the friendships between the characters. It is exciting and full of surprises. However, I think the story could be better if some of the side characters had more development, so we could learn more about their lives and motivations."
