Aqui estão os erros fonéticos captados na transcrição da sua leitura em comparação com o texto original:

1. **Texto original**: "He has spent his life working hard"
   **Transcrição**: "He has spent his life working hard"

   Correto.

2. **Texto original**: "He has been successful in business."
   **Transcrição**: "and has been successful in business."

   Erro: **"He has been"** transcrito como **"and has been"**.

3. **Texto original**: "He lives in a special and comfortable house."
   **Transcrição**: "He lives in a special and comfortable house."

   Correto.

4. **Texto original**: "He has many friends and he likes to enjoy"

   Correto.

   **Transcrição**: "with all his loved ones in hot weekends at home pool."

   Erro: **"at home"** transcrito como **"at home pool"**.

6. **Texto original**: "One of his favorite hobbies is traveling"
   **Transcrição**: "one of his favorite hobbies is traveling"

   Correto.

7. **Texto original**: "with his family."
   **Transcrição**: "with his family."

   Correto.

8. **Texto original**: "However, at 47 years old and weighing 127 kg"
   **Transcrição**: "however at 47 years old and the weigh in 127 kg"

   Erro: **"weighing"** transcrito como **"the weigh in"**.

9. **Texto original**: "he can no longer travel"
   **Transcrição**: "he can no longer travel"

   Correto.

    **Transcrição**: "today all his activities require"

    Correto.

    Erro: **"essential"** transcrito como **"precious accent"**, **"health"** como **"selfie"**.
